Lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagna is a oven baked dish , that involves baking large sheets of noodles with bolognaise sauce and cheese .

Ingredients

  • lasagna sheets
  • bolognaise sauce
  • riccota
  • eggs
  • Morzarrella
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

steps

  1. semi boil lasagna sheets
  2. mix riccota with eggs
  3. put olive oil in a pan and stir your bolonaise in it
  4. add the lasgana sheets,bolognaise and ricotto in layers
  5. add your shredded Morzarrella on top
  6. pre-heat your oven to 350 degrees
  7. Wrap your lasagna in foil and bake for 20 mins
  8. uncover your lasagna nd bake for another 10 mins for that beautiful crust
